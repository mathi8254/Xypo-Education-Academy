About Us

Xypo, established on December 31, 2003, stands out as a leading free online course provider, offering university-level education across diverse disciplines. With subjects spanning Business, Development, Design, Marketing, Photography, Web, and more, Xypo's courses feature pre-recorded video lectures for convenient, flexible learning. The platform's weekly subject sequence enhances user engagement, fostering a dynamic online learning community. Xypo breaks traditional barriers, providing learners with a broad range of courses and facilitating cost-efficient education. Through internet connectivity, teachers and students seamlessly interact, embodying the modern era of accessible and collaborative online education.
